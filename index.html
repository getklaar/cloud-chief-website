04 — About

THE
ARTIST

Cloud Chief is the musical universe of Chayen Wittmer, a Dutch producer and sound builder who constructs music from the ground up. Keys, guitar, his own recorded sounds, layered textures, and whatever the track asks for.

Since September 2025, AI has become part of the process, woven into his workflow alongside his own samples and creative direction. The result is a new kind of hybrid, where human instinct and machine speed meet somewhere in the middle and make something that sounds like neither.

Live, Cloud Chief blends live instrumentation with a DJ set. Warm, groove-heavy, the kind of set that feels like a late-night bar where nobody wants to leave.

"The record I always wanted to hear, but nobody made."

06 — Press & EPK

FOR THE
PRESS

Electronic Press Kit

Cloud Chief is the alias of Chayen Wittmer, a Dutch producer and sound builder from the Netherlands. He makes music from scratch, his own samples, keys, guitar, layered textures, and whatever the track asks for.

Since September 2025, AI has become part of his creative process, working alongside his own samples and musical instincts to build a new kind of sound. A hybrid approach where the human is still driving, just with some interesting tools on the passenger seat.

Live, Cloud Chief combines live instrumentation with a DJ set into something warm, groove-heavy and hard to leave early. Based in the Netherlands. Available for interviews, sync licensing and live bookings.
